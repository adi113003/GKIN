Loaded language

Repeated use of "backfired," "self-inflicted," "reckless" frames a contested policy outcome as settled fact before evidence is presented.

False cause

Treats the sanctions package as the sole driver of the energy crisis, omitting the weather and pipeline factors named in the reporting record (Finding 01).
