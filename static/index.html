Built for people who want the story behind the story.

GKIN does more than label content true or false. It surfaces the tactics, omissions, emotional framing, and narrative pattern shaping how a reader reacts.

C

Claim extraction

Pulls out verifiable claims and separates them from opinion, speculation, and unverifiable framing.

P

Persuasion map

Flags fear appeals, loaded language, urgency, authority framing, and other techniques with exact article spans.

M

Missing context

Shows what a reader needs to know before trusting the conclusion, not just whether the headline sounds plausible.

A

Assistant follow-up

Turns every scan into a conversation so users can question the evidence, compare narratives, and export reports.

From article to evidence trail in minutes.

The workflow is designed for fast reading, repeated use, and sharper judgment across messy information environments.

1

Bring the source

Paste article text, fetch a URL, upload media, or compare multiple pieces covering the same event.

2

Read the signals

Review manipulation score, emotional framing, authenticity signals, source checks, and narrative category.

3

Interrogate the result

Ask grounded follow-ups, run web research, save analyses, or export a report when the evidence matters.

Designed for the browser, not just a dashboard.

The included Chrome extension brings GKIN into the page a user is already reading, while the full workspace handles deeper analysis, comparisons, and reporting.
